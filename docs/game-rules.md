# Bhabhi Online - Game Rules (MVP)

## Players

- Exactly 5 players.
- Standard 52-card deck.
- No Jokers.

---

## Dealer

- Before the first game, every player draws one card.
- The player who draws the Ace becomes the first dealer.
- For future rounds, the dealer rotates clockwise.

---

## Dealing Cards

- Shuffle the deck.
- Deal all 52 cards.
- Every player receives 10 cards.
- The first 2 players receive one extra card (11 cards total).

---

## First Turn

- The player sitting to the right of the dealer starts the first trick.

---

## Playing Rules

The first card played determines the suit of the trick.

If a player has at least one card of that suit:

- They must play that suit.
- They may choose any card of that suit.
- They are NOT required to beat the highest card already played.

If a player does not have that suit:

- They may play any card.
- This is called a Thulla.

---

## Winning a Trick

- The highest card of the leading suit wins the trick.
- The winner starts the next trick.

---

## End of Game

The game continues until only one player still has cards.

That player becomes the Bhabhi (loser).

---

## Reconnection

Players may reconnect to an active game.

---

## MVP Scope

Included:

- Multiplayer
- 5 players
- Room system
- Reconnection
- AI support later

Not Included:

- Betting
- Gambling
- Coins
- Money
- Chat
